"Without a doubt using a Magni instead of a crane saves us on man hours, which saves us big on money. One of the big benefits is the 360-degree rotation. We used to put up forms and rebar with an excavator, but when you move with an excavator the forms move and sway. DISCOVER THE MAGNI ADVANTAGE

WORLD CLASS SAFETY

Magni's are safest in class machines. Features included a patented cab design allowing for greater visibility, load moment indicator (LMI) technology, the ability to set limits of critical movements ensuring no accidents, continuous 360-degree rotation (RTH models) reducing machine movement on the job site, and much more!

Superior Service and Support

Magni is all about making sure that your machines achieve maximum utilization. Spare parts are quickly dispatched from our two parts depots right here in the United States. Dealers are required to enroll in our world class service training programs. Magni has many factory trained service support personnel in the United States to support our dealers and you as needed.

Multiple Machines in One

With their ability to rotate continuously 360-degrees and the availability of over 100 attachments, Magni provides you with several machines in one. Having a machine that can take the place of several on a jobsite saves you on equipment expenses, operators, fuel, insurance, and logistical costs. You won't need to rent or buy a forklift, crane and MEWP when you can do it all with a Magni!

Save Time. Save Money.

At Magni we take the time to fully understand your needs specific to your trade. Then we design and engineer our machines and attachments to not only meet them, but to allow you to operate safer and faster resulting in increased profit for you! Contractors regularly report up to 35% gains in efficiency using Magni's.

Easy to Use and Learn

Operators consistently report that Magni's are both easy to learn and simple to use. They feel safer and operate more efficiently. In fact, most operators don't want to ever give their Magni back!

STRONGEST & HIGHEST REACHING Telehandlers

Magni's goal has always been to redefine the market in order to give you the advantages that you need to succeed. Our machines lift higher, heavier and safer than the competition. About Magni

The future of lifting technology

"Quality, craftmanship and innovation. The secret lies in precision. Having clear ideas is vital: we need to know where we want to go and what we want to create. And at Magni, we throw our all into accomplishing it." Riccardo Magni - President

Founded in 2013, Magni has quickly become the leader in the development of the widest and most dynamic range of telehandlers in the world, from the highest lifting rotating telescopic handlers, to the heaviest capacity, heavy duty models, to our uniquely functioning fixed-boom construction telescopic handlers. Magni's are produced at our state-of-the-art facilities in Italy and supported by our six branches worldwide. Our ultimate commitment to world class technical service and parts has led us to partner with over 300 dealers worldwide.
